Severstal Steel Defect Detection

This GitHub Pages website provides information about the Severstal Steel Defect Detection project.

Overview

Steel is one of the most important building materials of modern times. Steel buildings are resistant to natural and man-made wear, making the material ubiquitous globally. To make steel production more efficient, this competition aims to identify defects.

Severstal, a leader in efficient steel mining and production, is using machine learning to improve automation, increase efficiency, and maintain high quality in their production. The company has created the countryâ€™s largest industrial data lake and is now looking to the machine learning community for help in localizing and classifying surface defects on a steel sheet.

If successful, the competition will contribute to keeping manufacturing standards for steel high and enable Severstal to continue their innovation, leading to a stronger, more efficient world.
